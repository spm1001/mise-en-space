The httpx migration revealed that the adapter layer has two strata: the six "data" adapters (docs, drive, sheets, slides, calendar, activity) that feed the fetch/search tools, and three "infrastructure" adapters (charts.py, conversion.py, gmail.py) that are structurally different. Charts creates and destroys temporary Slides presentations — it's orchestration, not data fetching. Conversion does the same with Drive uploads. Gmail uses batch requests. All three still import from services.py and can't be migrated with the same mechanical pattern. When step 8 says "remove services.py", it really means "migrate charts + conversion + gmail first, then remove." The five tools that import get_drive_service/get_docs_service directly (mise-tadigo) are a third stratum — they bypass the adapter layer entirely.
