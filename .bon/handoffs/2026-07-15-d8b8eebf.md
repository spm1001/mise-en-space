## For Claudes to come

**Rotating an OAuth credential in a GCP project can silently kill OTHER clients that authorize through that project — including Apps Script's auto-created trigger clients — and the failure is invisible.** The Email Attachment Extractor's ongoing trigger went dark for ~4 months (Error 401 deleted_client) because a workspace-MCP OAuth-cred rotation deleted the Apps Script's own auto-created client in the shared project. Nothing surfaced it — no Google disable-email, no log; the only evidence was the *product* (a frozen lastRun cursor / missing Drive month-folders). Before rotating a project's OAuth creds, audit what else authorizes through it (background Apps Script triggers especially). This is now the durable "monitor the product, not the proxy" lesson made concrete, and it lives in understanding.md's apps-script subsystem section along with the full operational map (the never-deployed-refactor trap, the deploy recon, the scripts.run-can't-create-triggers rule, and the itv-appscript gotchas).
